# Privacy Policy for ChanryTabStash

**Last Updated:** April 2026

ChanryTabStash ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our Chrome Extension.

## 1. Data We Access and How We Use It
ChanryTabStash is a productivity tool designed strictly to efficiently manage, categorize, and organize your browser tabs. To function properly, the extension accesses:

* **Web History (Tab URLs and Titles):** We read the URLs and titles of your currently open browser tabs exclusively to display them in the extension's interface and group them.
* **API Keys:** To utilize the "AI Auto Categorize" feature, users provide their own Google Gemini API Key.

## 2. Where Your Data Is Stored
* **Local Storage Only:** Both your Gemini API Key and your saved tab session data are stored securely and **exclusively in your browser's local storage**.
* **No Server Collection:** We do not operate any backend servers. We never collect, harvest, transmit, or store any of your personal data, web history, or API keys on our own servers. 

## 3. Third-Party Services (Google Gemini AI)
When you actively click the "AI Auto Categorize" feature, the extension transmits the titles and URLs of your currently selected tabs directly to the **Google Gemini API** for the sole purpose of semantic organization. Your API requests are processed strictly under your own account API key and governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

## 4. Limited Use Disclosure
Our use of the data you grant us access to complies fully with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies regarding Limited Use. We guarantee that:
* We will never sell or transfer your data to third parties.
* We will not use or transfer your user data for purposes unrelated to the item's single purpose.
* We will not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository: `https://github.com/chanryTW/chanrytabstash`
